Our Story

Inspired by a trusted family calendar, the iMediate app was born from the idea that co-parenting tools should feel as natural, visible, and reliable as the paper planners we once relied on.

Long before iMediate, our founder's family relied on a giant paper calendar.
It hung in the kitchen as a single source of truth for everyone’s schedules.
An Aunt gifted their mom a new one every Christmas.
No app felt as natural, or as visible, as that planner with it's layers of handwriting.
When researching parenting tools, our team kept returning to that kitchen wall calendar.
As Families evolved. Communication did too.

Listening to Real Needs

iMediate goes beyond tech. Offering secure, discreet data-logging to help parents document interactions, rebuild trust, and navigate legal challenges with confidence.

Two-home households exposed the paper system’s limits.
What if an app could turn conversations into calendar events?
That idea became our north star! What would we need to do this?
But we didn’t stop at tech solutions, we listened to stories.
We heard from parents, mediators, and legal professionals.
Behind the scenes were patterns of coercion, confusion, and conflict.

Why iMediate Matters

iMediate brings peace of mind and consistency to parenting through simple, smart, and privacy-first design; like the paper planner, reimagined.

Parents needed a secure way to document interactions; quietly and confidently.
iMediate is about consistency and peace of mind...
For parents. And most importantly for their children.
It is the "kitchen calendar" reimagined with smart automation.
And built from the ground up for privacy and trust.
A parenting companion that feels natural and indispensable.
Welcome to a new era of co-parenting.
Welcome to iMediate.
